Strategy 1

Keep rolling until you get 20 points, or "hold 20". Optimal when players have low scores, i.e. at the start.

Strategy 2

Consider the other player's score. If there is a big score gap and you are losing, roll more during your turn.

Strategy 3

If you are ahead of your opponent, consider whether you can go ahead and win during a given turn.
