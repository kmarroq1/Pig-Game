Pig Game Instructions

Common Game Variations

When a player rolls two dice and one of the die is a 1, they lose the points for that turn. If they rolls snake eyes, they lose their total score.

When a player rolls two dice and one of the die is a 1, they lose the points for that turn. If they roll snake eyes, they add 25 to their score, but must roll again.

Can be played with 1 or 2 dice, as above, but with six as the bad number, instead of 1. Similarly, you can choose to substitute any number for 1.

The player rolls only once per turn with an any number of dice. Can also apply other game rules, but with many dice.

Players begin the round standing. During the round, a player may hold by sitting. The round continues until all players are sitting or a 1 is rolled. There are five rounds total, for each letter in SKUNK. The highest score after five rounds wins.
