History of Pig

Pig is a folk jeopardy dice game first found in the literature of John Scarne in 1945. Scarne, an American magician and author, described the basic rules as players rolling a die until you roll a 1 or you decide to pass, or "hold", your turn. Due to its complex dynamics, Pig, and its many adaptations have many classroom applications, like in math classes and computer science courses.
